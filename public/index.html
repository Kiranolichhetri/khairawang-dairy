About Us

Our Story of Pure
Dairy Excellence

For over two decades, KHAIRAWANG DAIRY has been committed to delivering the freshest and finest dairy products to families across Nepal. Our family-owned farm nestled in the pristine hills follows traditional methods combined with modern quality standards.

Every product we create is a testament to our dedication to quality, sustainability, and the well-being of our community. From our happy, grass-fed cows to your table, we ensure each step maintains the highest standards of freshness and nutrition.
