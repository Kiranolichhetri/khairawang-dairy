25+ Years of Excellence

From Humble Beginnings to Your Table

KHAIRAWANG DAIRY started in 1999 as a small family dairy farm in the beautiful hills of Khairawang, Rupandehi. What began as a passion project to share pure, fresh dairy with our neighbors has grown into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of families across Nepal.

Our commitment remains unchanged: to deliver the freshest, most nutritious dairy products while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability. Every product we create is a testament to our dedication to excellence.
